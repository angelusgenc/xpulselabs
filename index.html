CSV import explained

Build post lists with ChatGPT or any other AI

One of the fastest ways to use X Pulse Pro is to create a batch of post ideas with ChatGPT or another AI tool, then place them into a CSV file and import them into the app in seconds.

  • Ask an AI tool to generate 20, 50, or 100 posts around your niche or product.
  • Copy those posts into a simple CSV file so each row becomes one post.
  • Use the Add from CSV button inside X Pulse Pro to load them quickly.
  • Review, edit, reorder, and start your schedule without manually pasting one post at a time.
Example workflow: “Write me 30 short X posts about productivity software” → export to CSV → import into X Pulse Pro → schedule and run.

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers for the things people usually want to know before downloading or buying.

What exactly does the CSV import do?

It lets you bring in many prepared posts at once instead of adding them one by one. This is especially useful when you generate content ideas with ChatGPT or another AI and want to load them into the app quickly.

Can I edit posts after importing them?

Yes. Importing is only the starting point. You can still review, adjust, reorder, or delete posts from your list before running your schedule.

Do I need to enter my X username or password?

No. X Pulse Pro does not ask for your X username or password inside the app. You simply keep X open in your browser session and run the workflow from there.

Does it support media posts too?

Yes. The interface includes a separate media post workflow so you can mix text and media content more naturally.

Is this for beginners or advanced users?

Both. The layout is simple enough to understand quickly, but it is still useful for people who want a more efficient, repeatable content routine.
